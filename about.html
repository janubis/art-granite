FAQ

How long does installation take?

Most countertop installations are completed in one day. The entire process from selection to installation typically takes 3–5 days.

How do I care for my granite or marble?

We provide detailed care instructions with every installation. Generally, regular cleaning with mild soap and periodic sealing keeps your stone beautiful.
